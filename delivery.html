Delivery Service.

At Stuckey's, we know that convenience is what it's all about. Whether you make your purchase over the phone or at our location, we'll make sure it's never a problem to get it home.

We offer delivery service for any order, no matter how large or small, and we have the equipment to make that delivery convenient for you.

So whether you order a large pallet of stone that requires a forklift, or a cubic yard of mulch, we can make sure it arrives at your location without a hassle.

Our delivery charges are based on the distance we have to travel to your location, and also depend on the equipment required.
