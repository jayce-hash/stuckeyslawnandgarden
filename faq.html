Does mulch attract termites?

No, there is no kind of mulch that attracts termites. In general, every acre of land (in this area of the country) contains colonies of termites; the presence of mulch neither attracts nor causes them. Termites require wood of at least a 2" thickness to build their nests. As a result, mulch is not sufficient for their needs and they will not nest in it.

How many tons are in a yard of gravel?

When we talk about a "yard" of gravel (or other material), we are really referring to a cubic yard. There are approximately 1.25 to 1.5 tons in a cubic yard of gravel.
